Customs

Customs clearance
intermediation from and to
Macedonia

Freight forwarding primarily includes the process of customs clearance
intermediation of goods during import, export, or re-export from and to
Macedonia.


Simply put, if you want to purchase goods from abroad, to be used in
Macedonia, or if you have goods that you want to sell from Macedonia abroad,
you need a freight forwarder.


If you are interested in a more detailed explanation of customs procedures,
please do not hesitate to contact us.
